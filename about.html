About

Rough House is a well-established video production company with post production & finishing at our core. Our comfortable suites in the heart of SOMA provide editorial, color, finishing and visual effects. We've added a state-of-the-art audio suite to extend our finishing capabilities.

We work with clients on everything from production to creative editorial and finishing. Our skilled team has the experience and production savvy to help bring your ideas to life. This range of services allow us to successfully integrate with our partners across a wide gamut of projects.

We also offer virtual color services with world renowned artists. This allows our clients access to some of the best colorist from around the globe in addition to our established local resources.

Thanks for visiting, we look forward to collaborating.
